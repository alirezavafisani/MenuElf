Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

MenuElf ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by Alireza Vafisani. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MenuElf mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address, used for authentication and account recovery.
  • Food preferences: Answers to our onboarding taste quiz and preferences learned from your usage, including cuisine preferences, dietary restrictions, spice tolerance, and price sensitivity.
  • Chat messages: Messages you send to the MenuElf AI assistant, used to generate personalized responses and refine your taste profile.
  • Friend connections: Your username, display name, avatar, and friend list, used to power social features and group dining plans.
  • Location data: Your approximate location, used to show nearby restaurants on the map. Location is only accessed when you use the map feature and is not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalize restaurant and dish recommendations based on your taste profile.
  • Power AI chat conversations that understand your food preferences.
  • Connect you with friends for group dining planning.
  • Show nearby restaurants on the map.
  • Improve the quality of our recommendations over time.

3. Third-Party Services

MenuElf uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication, database storage, and user management.
  • OpenAI: Powers the AI chat assistant and dish search.
  • Google Maps: Provides map display and location services.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, your taste profile, chat history, friend connections, and all personal data will be permanently removed.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, solely for the purpose of providing app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

MenuElf is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will notify you through an app update. The updated policy will be effective upon your continued use of the app after the update.
