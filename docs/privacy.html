MenuElf Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

MenuElf is a restaurant menu discovery app for Calgary. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Location Data: MenuElf uses your device location to show nearby restaurants. Location data is processed on your device and not stored on our servers.

Chat Messages: When you use the AI chat feature, messages are sent to our server for responses. We do not permanently store conversations.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identification information collected
  • No account creation or login required
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No data sold to third parties

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: Powers AI chat
  • Google Maps: Displays the map

Data Security

All communication is encrypted using HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

MenuElf is not directed at children under 13.
